Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how DentAIstudy (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Who we are

DentAIstudy is an online platform that provides AI-powered study tools for dental students and professionals worldwide.

2. Information we collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: name, email address, and authentication details when you create an account.
  • Usage data: interactions with study tools, preferences, and activity history within the platform.
  • Payment-related data: transaction references, plan type, and billing country. We do not store full card details.
  • Technical data: IP address, device type, browser, and basic analytics data.

3. How payments are handled

Payments are processed securely by third-party payment providers. DentAIstudy does not store or process full payment card details. Payment providers may collect information required to complete transactions and prevent fraud.

4. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the DentAIstudy service
  • Manage user accounts and subscriptions
  • Process payments and send invoices or receipts
  • Improve platform performance and user experience
  • Communicate service updates or important notices
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

5. Legal basis for processing (EU/UK users)

If you are located in the EU or UK, we process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Your consent
  • Performance of a contract (providing the service)
  • Legal obligations (tax, accounting, compliance)
  • Legitimate interests (service improvement and security)

6. Data sharing

We only share personal data with trusted service providers where necessary, including:

  • Authentication and hosting providers
  • Payment processing partners
  • Tax and accounting service providers

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

7. Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal requirements. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Restriction or objection to processing
  • Data portability

To exercise your rights, contact us at info@dentaistudy.com.

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate the platform and improve user experience. Details are available in our Cookies Policy.

10. International users

DentAIstudy operates globally. Your data may be processed or stored outside your country of residence, with appropriate safeguards in place.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

12. Contact

If you have questions about privacy or data protection, contact us at: info@dentaistudy.com
