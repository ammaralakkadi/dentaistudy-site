Yes — DentAIstudy’s core study tools are currently free to use. You can generate OSCE flows, notes, and flashcards without any subscription. As we grow, we may introduce optional premium features, but the essential study experience will remain accessible.
DentAIstudy is built specifically for dental students — not as a general chatbot, but as a structured study workspace. It focuses on exam-ready outputs such as OSCE checklists, viva points, classifications, and clear clinical reasoning. Instead of long paragraphs, you get clean, organized study material designed the way examiners think.
DentAIstudy is designed for studying, revision, and strengthening your understanding of dental concepts. It is not a clinical decision tool and should never replace supervisors, professors, or official guidelines. Always verify information during real patient care.
You can explore the platform without an account. Creating an account unlocks extra benefits such as saving study packs, tracking your activity, and personalizing your study workspace.
DentAIstudy supports all major dental subjects, including Operative, Endodontics, Prosthodontics, Orthodontics, Periodontics, Oral Surgery, Oral Pathology, Radiology, and more. We continuously refine and expand coverage to keep your study materials comprehensive and up to date.
We store only the information needed to run your DentAIstudy account and keep your study materials available across devices. Your data is private, never sold, and protected under our Privacy Policy. You’re always in control of your content.
DentAIstudy is designed for undergraduate dental students, interns/house officers, and postgraduate trainees who want structured, examiner-style study material in a fast, distraction-free workspace.
