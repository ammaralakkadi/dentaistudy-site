Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 18 December 2025

This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains how refunds, cancellations, and subscription changes are handled for DentAIstudy. By purchasing a paid subscription, you agree to the terms below.

1. Subscription model

DentAIstudy provides digital, subscription-based access to AI-powered educational tools. Subscriptions are billed on a recurring monthly or yearly basis, as shown on the Pricing page.

2. 14-day right of withdrawal

You may request a refund within 14 days of your initial purchase, in accordance with applicable consumer protection laws.

Access to digital content begins immediately after purchase. Refunds within the 14-day period may be declined in cases of misuse or repeated abuse.

3. How to request a refund

To request a refund, contact us at info@dentaistudy.com within 14 days of purchase and include:

  • The email address used for your DentAIstudy account
  • The date of purchase
  • The reason for your refund request (optional but helpful)

4. Refund processing

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. Processing times depend on your payment provider and bank, and may take several business days to appear on your statement.

5. Renewals & recurring charges

Subscription renewals are charged automatically at the end of each billing period. Renewal payments are generally non-refundable once processed, unless required by law.

6. Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Cancellation stops future billing but does not automatically issue a refund for the current billing period.

After cancellation, you will retain access until the end of your current paid period.

7. Abuse & exceptional cases

We reserve the right to deny refund requests in cases of misuse, repeated refund abuse, or violation of our Terms & Conditions.

8. Contact

For any questions about refunds or cancellations, contact us at: info@dentaistudy.com
