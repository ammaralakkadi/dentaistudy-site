Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 10 December 2025

This Refund Policy explains how refunds are handled for purchases made on DentAIstudy. By subscribing to any plan, you agree to the terms below.

1. Dodo Payments as Merchant of Record

All payments, subscription charges, and applicable taxes are securely processed by our reseller and Merchant of Record, Dodo Payments. This means Dodo Payments manages invoicing, billing, and refund processing where required by law.

2. 14-Day Refund Window

DentAIstudy offers a 14-day refund period for all new purchases. If you are not satisfied with your subscription, you may request a full refund within 14 days of the original transaction date.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at info@dentaistudy.com. Include the email used for your account and your Dodo Payments receipt if available. Eligible refunds will be issued by Dodo Payments back to your original payment method.

4. Renewals & Recurring Charges

Subscription renewals are non-refundable once the renewal has been processed. To avoid future charges, you may cancel your subscription at any time before the renewal date from your account settings.

5. Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future billing but does not trigger an automatic refund unless requested within the 14-day window.

6. Exceptions

  • Refunds requested after 14 days from the original purchase are generally not eligible.
  • Promotional or discounted offers may not be refundable unless required by law.
  • Abuse of the platform, violation of terms, or excessive refund requests may void eligibility.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact: info@dentaistudy.com
