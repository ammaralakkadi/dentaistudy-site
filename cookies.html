Cookies Policy

Last updated: 18 November 2025

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small files placed on your device to help websites function properly, remember your preferences, and improve stability and security.

2. How DentAIstudy uses cookies

DentAIstudy currently uses only essential cookies needed for:

  • keeping you logged in,
  • maintaining platform security,
  • enabling basic navigation,
  • ensuring core features operate normally.

Other cookie types (may be added later)

  • Preference cookies – to save settings like language or theme.
  • Performance cookies – simple analytics to understand feature usage.

3. No advertising or tracking cookies

DentAIstudy does not use ad cookies, tracking pixels, or marketing scripts. We do not sell or share cookie data with third‑party advertisers.

4. Local storage

Some features may use your browser’s local storage to save lightweight preferences. This information stays on your device and is not shared externally.

5. Managing cookies

You may control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note:

  • Disabling essential cookies can break core functionality.
  • Your login session may not be saved.
  • Clearing browser data resets local storage preferences.

6. Updates to this policy

If DentAIstudy adds new cookie types (such as analytics), this page will be updated and, where required, consent will be requested before using them.
