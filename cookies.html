Cookies Policy

Last updated: 10 December 2025

This Cookies Policy explains how DentAIstudy (“we”, “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies to provide and improve the platform. By using the Service, you agree to the use of cookies as described below.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help websites function properly, keep you signed in, remember preferences, and understand basic usage patterns.

2. How We Use Cookies

DentAIstudy uses a minimal set of cookies required to operate the platform. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

We use cookies for:

  • Authentication: to keep your session active when you log in.
  • Security: to protect your account and prevent unauthorized access.
  • Preferences: to remember your study level, selected modes, or site settings.
  • Analytics: anonymous usage analytics that help us improve features and performance.

3. Cookies Used by Supabase

DentAIstudy uses Supabase for authentication and secure session management. Supabase may set essential cookies to verify your identity and maintain your login session. These cookies are strictly necessary for the Service to function.

4. No Advertising or Tracking Cookies

We do not use advertising networks, retargeting scripts, or behavioral tracking cookies. Your data is never shared with ad platforms.

5. Managing Cookies

You may control or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may prevent you from logging in or accessing core features of the platform.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy periodically. Continued use of the Service after changes indicates acceptance of the updated version.

7. Contact

If you have questions about cookies or privacy, contact us at: info@dentaistudy.com.
