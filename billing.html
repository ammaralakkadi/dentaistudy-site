Billing Policy

Last updated: 18 December 2025

This Billing Policy explains how subscriptions, renewals, and payments are handled for DentAIstudy. By subscribing to any paid plan, you agree to the terms below.

1. Payments & billing partner

Payments for DentAIstudy are processed securely through our payment provider. During checkout, you may be redirected to a hosted payment page to complete your purchase. Your bank statement may show a charge from our payment provider.

2. Taxes

Where required by law, applicable taxes (such as VAT, GST, or sales tax) may be calculated and added at checkout based on your location and tax status. If you provide a valid VAT ID (where applicable), taxes may be handled accordingly.

3. Subscription billing

DentAIstudy operates on a subscription model. When you purchase a paid plan, you are granted access for the duration of the billing period (monthly or yearly). Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled before the renewal date.

4. Pricing

Current prices for all plans are displayed on the Pricing page. Prices may change in the future, but active subscribers will always be notified in advance if a change affects their plan.

5. Automatic renewals

At the end of each billing period, your subscription will renew automatically using the payment method you provided at checkout. Renewal charges are non-refundable once processed, except where required by law or as described in our Refund Policy.

6. Invoices & receipts

After each successful payment, you will receive a receipt or invoice by email. This document may include tax information where applicable.

7. Failed payments

If a payment fails, we (or our payment provider) may retry the charge. If retries continue to fail, your subscription may be paused until billing information is updated.

8. Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Cancellation stops future billing but does not automatically issue a refund. Refunds must be requested separately and are subject to DentAIstudy’s Refund Policy.

9. Upgrades & downgrades

  • Upgrades: If you upgrade to a higher plan, the change may apply immediately. Any prorating (if used) will be shown during checkout or in your account.
  • Downgrades: Downgrades typically take effect at the next renewal date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about billing, please contact us at info@dentaistudy.com.
